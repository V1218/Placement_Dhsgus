Recruitment Procedure

The step-by-step process for recruitment is as follows:

  1. Email your recruitment interest to placementcell@dhsgu.edu.in.
  2. Fill out the Job Notification Form (JNF).
  3. Schedule pre-placement talks and discussions.
  4. Coordinate interviews with the Placement Cell.
  5. Finalize candidate selection and share the results with the Placement Cell.
