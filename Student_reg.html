Placement Opportunities for Students

Dr. Hari Singh Gour University (DHSGSU), Sagar, offers excellent placement opportunities for students across various disciplines. The university has strong industry connections, allowing students to interact with top companies and secure internships and full-time positions. The dedicated Placement Cell provides comprehensive support, including resume-building workshops, mock interviews, and pre-placement talks, ensuring students are well-prepared for the competitive job market.

In addition to academic training, DHSGSU emphasizes holistic development, offering skill-building programs in communication, leadership, and teamwork. The university's diverse student body brings a variety of perspectives, making DHSGSU graduates highly sought after by employers. With a focus on both technical and soft skills, DHSGSU ensures its students are equipped to excel in their chosen careers, making it an ideal destination for those looking to kickstart their professional journey.

Rules and Guidelines for Students in Placement Cell - DHSGSU

  • Eligibility Criteria: Students must meet the academic performance criteria set by the Placement Cell to participate in the recruitment process.
  • Registration for Placement: Students must complete the online registration with accurate details within the specified timeframe.
  • Resume Submission: An updated and professional resume must be submitted to the Placement Cell before the recruitment process begins.
  • Attendance in Pre-Placement Activities: Students must attend all mandatory pre-placement activities like workshops, mock interviews, and seminars.
  • Punctuality: Students must arrive on time for all placement-related events; tardiness may result in disqualification.
  • Dress Code: Students must follow a professional dress code, wearing formal business attire for interviews and placement activities.
  • Behavior and Professionalism: Students must maintain professionalism and respect towards recruiters, fellow students, and Placement Cell staff at all times.
  • No-Show Policy: Students must inform the Placement Cell in advance if unable to attend scheduled interviews or placement events.
  • Multiple Offers: Students must inform the Placement Cell immediately after accepting a job offer and show integrity in managing multiple offers.
  • Confidentiality: Students must maintain confidentiality regarding interview details and any proprietary information shared by recruiters.
  • Social Media Conduct: Students should ensure their social media profiles reflect professionalism, avoiding inappropriate content that could affect their placement chances.
  • Communication with Placement Cell: Students should promptly communicate any queries or updates with the Placement Cell through official channels.
  • Feedback and Suggestions: Students are encouraged to provide constructive feedback on the placement process to help improve future placement activities.
  • Disqualification: Violating any of the above rules may lead to disqualification from the placement process for that academic year.
